# 学术汇报、开题中期与毕业答辩 / Academic Presentation, Proposal Defense, and Final Defense

## Summary / 简介
This pack organizes presentation work by scenario rather than slide aesthetics, so the speaker can decide what to say, to whom, and at what depth.

这个知识包按汇报场景而不是按幻灯片模板来组织，帮助用户识别‘要讲什么、给谁讲、讲到多深’。

## Frameworks / 方法框架
1. **Audience-Question-Evidence**: Choose slides and speaking depth by what the audience needs to understand or challenge.
1. **Defense Scenario Routing**: Proposal, progress review, and final defense each require different levels of certainty and scope.

## Expanded Workflow / 扩展工作流
1. 先定义这次汇报要达成的决策目标。
2. 按 audience-question-evidence 三元组安排材料。
3. 把讲稿分成‘必须放在 slides 上’和‘适合口头展开’两层。
4. 预设最容易被追问的几个环节，提前准备证据与解释。
5. 答辩前做一次时间版 rehearsal，保证主线不散。

## Decision Rules / 判断规则
- 不同答辩场景的目标不同，结构也不同。
- 讲稿要服务评审问题，而不是服务你想展示的一切努力。
- slides 负责结构和证据，口头负责转场和解释。

## Checklists / 检查清单
- What is the core decision this defense is trying to secure?
- What evidence must the audience trust before moving on?
- What belongs in slides versus the spoken explanation?

## Common Pitfalls / 常见误区
- 把答辩做成材料堆砌
- 每页都信息过满，导致主线不清
- 只练表达，不练问题应对
- 不同场景套用同一套 slides
